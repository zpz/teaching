Suppose $X$ has pdf
\[
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{8\pi}}\exp\Bigl(-(x-\mu)^2/8\Bigr),
\qquad
-\infty < x < \infty
\]
Given an i.i.d.\@ sample
$X_1,\dotsc,X_n$ of $X$,
find the maximum likelihood estimator for $\mu$.
